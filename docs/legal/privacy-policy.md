# Privacy Policy

**Version 1.0**  
**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

APIMarketplace Pro ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, organization name
- **Payment Information**: Billing address, payment method details (processed by Stripe)
- **Profile Information**: Avatar, bio, contact preferences
- **API Information**: For providers - API documentation, specifications, pricing
- **Support Communications**: Messages, tickets, feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, click patterns
- **API Usage Data**: Request counts, endpoints called, response times, error rates
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error logs, security events
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

- **OAuth Providers**: If you sign in with Google/GitHub, we receive basic profile information
- **GitHub Integration**: Repository metadata for codebase analytics (with your permission)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Delivery

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
- Process transactions and send billing notifications
- Authenticate users and prevent fraud
- Monitor API usage and enforce rate limits
- Generate analytics and usage reports

### 3.2 Communication

- Send transactional emails (invoices, password resets, account notifications)
- Respond to support requests
- Send service announcements and updates
- Deliver marketing communications (with your consent)

### 3.3 Improvement and Development

- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Develop new products and services
- Conduct research and analytics
- Train AI models (on anonymized data only)

### 3.4 Security and Legal Compliance

- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and property

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 With Your Consent

We share information when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

### 4.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **Vercel**: Hosting and CDN
- **Anthropic**: AI features (code generation, assistance)
- **Email Service**: Transactional emails (e.g., Resend, SendGrid)
- **Analytics**: Aggregate usage analytics

These providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

### 4.3 API Providers

When you subscribe to an API, we share:
- Your organization name (not personal email)
- Subscription tier and usage data
- Support inquiries related to their API

### 4.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 4.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Protect the safety of users or the public

### 4.6 Aggregate Data

We may share anonymized, aggregate data that does not identify you personally (e.g., "50% of users use feature X").

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

### 5.2 Deleted Accounts

After account deletion, we retain:
- **Immediate deletion**: Passwords, API keys, personal contact details
- **90 days**: User-generated content, session data
- **7 years**: Financial records (invoices, payment transactions) for legal/tax compliance
- **Indefinitely**: Anonymized usage analytics

### 5.3 Legal Holds

We may retain information longer if required by legal hold, investigation, or litigation.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

### 6.2 Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).

### 6.4 Right to Restriction

Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### 6.6 Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for processing activities that require consent.

### 6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

**To exercise these rights**, visit your account Privacy Settings or contact privacy@apimarketplace.pro.

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Technical Measures

- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Hashing**: Passwords hashed with bcrypt, API keys hashed with SHA-256
- **Monitoring**: Real-time security monitoring and incident response

### 7.2 Organizational Measures

- Employee training on data protection
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Data breach response plan
- Privacy by design principles

### 7.3 No Guarantee

While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

### 8.1 Cookie Types

- **Analytics Cookies**: Usage statistics (optional, respects your preferences)
- **Marketing Cookies**: Ad targeting and attribution (optional)

### 8.2 Cookie Management

You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie banner or browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality.

### 8.3 Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals and will not track users who enable DNT.
